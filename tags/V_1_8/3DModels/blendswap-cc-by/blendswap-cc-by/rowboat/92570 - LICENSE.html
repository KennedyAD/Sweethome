About "Kayak and Rowboat (Dinghy)":

  • Blender: 2.79
  • Render Engine: Cycles
  • Uploaded on: 2018-10-01 17:54:54
This single seater kayak is fully featured for close-up scenes. The only part that's kind of rudimentary is the adjustable footrest -- everything else is as shown on different kayak models on the internet. Same with the rowboat.There's one thing with the rowboat that wants looking into if you plan to move the oars in either craft and that's the meshes that connect to the oars; they're not properly rigged so that the meshes move together correctly. Someone with more experience than I will have to work that out. The parts of the oarlock are, however, parented together in a way that I hope makes it easier to rig.All parts have intelligent names and the model is grouped and parented for easy appending or linking to your scene.
